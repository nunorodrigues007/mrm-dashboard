Current Reading: 29 basis points | Score: 9.0/10 | ERP Sentinel: ACTIVE

This reading triggered our ERP Sentinel threshold, signaling that forward equity returns are statistically unlikely to compensate for embedded volatility.

Implication: Any reversion toward fair-value ERP (historically 300-400 bps) implies material multiple contraction. Position sizing in equity beta should reflect this mathematical reality.
